insights& opportunities

External

Opportunity:

“To be more open and transparent and in that way attract new members”

Insights:
Young people don’t know that much about Rotary which gives us a freedom in creating that communication. Rotary has the possibility to increase their communication of different members accomplishments in volunteering. There’s a risk that Rotary's ambition to find highly talented and influentious people gives potential members a feeling that they’re not good enough to become members.
How Might We?

...Make Rotary less intimidating?
...Communicate the good value in networking + volunteering?
...Make Rotary a more open and inclusive organisation?
...Share and leverage skills and accomplishments of individual members?
...Use social media to increase transparency?

Opportunity:

“Encourage people with initiative by sharing the power of Rotary”

Insights:
There’s a risk that Rotarys combination of volunteering and networking may be percieved as not very trustworthy.
How Might We?

...Give more power and purpose to potential members?
...Make networking + volunteering a trustworthy combination?
...Create challenges to trigger ideas and attract ambitious people?
...Make Rotary look appealing as a business opportunity?
...Help potential members fulfill themselves through Rotary projects?
...Extend Rotary's Network outside of Rotary?
...Match Rotary resources with people who want to do good?
...Invite people to collaborate without strings attached?
...Share the knowledge of Rotary with the world?

Opportunity:

“To lower the barrier of entrance by enabling smaller commitments”

Insights:
Asking something small initially is a good way to get someone to take a bigger step later on. There’s a high barrier of entry into Rotary.
How Might We?

...Change the strategy of which Rotary approaches members?
...Reach members who are ready to be involved and engaged?
...Make Rotary more transparent and inclusive?
...Create attractive projects for members of the target group?
...Find projects to address issues the new target group cares about?

Internal

Opportunity:

“Make Rotary into a network that brings more value to the target group”

Insights:
Rotary has a huge network. If we could leverage that in an effective way this would strengthen Rotary as a brand and network.
How Might We?

...Move members from Rotaract and Activate to Rotary?
...Improve communication between clubs?
...Improve networking between clubs?
...Bring more value to local communities through club collaboration?
...Improve the Rotary network through digital solutions?

Opportunity:

“Offer a more flexible membership, to bring value to the changing needs of a member”

Insights:
The needs of a member in Rotary changes over time. For example networking and career improvement opportunities are important for younger members while volunteering is more important for older members. Rotary needs to be able to cater to different needs to make members lifetime companions.
How Might We?

...Make becoming a member a more open process without compromising the value of the Rotary brand?
...Tailor the strategy to the changing needs of the members?
...Exchange knowledge in a more efficient way?
...Understand what a flexible membership means to members?
..Make the information find members, and not the other way around?
...Make it easier to be a part of Rotary?
...Create a valuable relationship with members during the first three years?

Opportunity:

“Use the mentorship program to find new members”

Insights:
Rotary has to give something valuable in the mentor program to get as much value back from the program as possible.
How Might We?

...Find out what people want in a mentor, and then match that with the right mentor?
...Find partners interested in the mentorship program?
...Find people interested in having a mentor?
...Make the program valuable for mentees?
...Make the mentorship process valuable for mentors?
...Present the mentorship program in an attractive way?
