Social Media Guidelines

Social media has made it easier than ever to gain access to peoples opinions and problems through the rise of social media. Instead of looking internally to Rotarians for insights into defining the best ways to improve society based on their individual opinions and areas of professional expertise, Rotary has the opportunity to serve society in a new way by inviting people to share their opinions and talk about their problems, and how Rotary can best meet these needs.
