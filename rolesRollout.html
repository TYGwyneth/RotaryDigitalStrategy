Roles

Community Manager

Rotary Sweden

On national level, Rotary Sweden should appoint a Community Manager, which would have the following areas of responsibility:

- Overall responsible for implementing digital strategy throughout Rotary clubs.

- Creating themes for Rotary in social media. What should Rotary and its clubs talk about and for how long? Then communicating these themes with instructions and content to the clubs.

- Content creation for sharing on social medias on both national and club level.

Social Media Responsible

Club Level

Each club should appoint one member to be generally responsible for the social media presence for his/her club. This role will include responsibility to share content created by the Community Manager at a regional level as well as creating coherent sharable content on club level.

- Responsible for implementing digital guidelines on club level.

- Administrator over facebook group and pages, aswell as any other platform the club chooses to be present on.

- Sharing content in a coherent way, both given to them from the community manager as well as locally created content.

IT Implementation

Open Member Search

To reach out with its member search system Rotary needs to appoint resources towards developing an open platform based on this system. See the section about Resource Reach for more information on how this could be done.
